## About The App

<div align="center"> <img src="valuex.gif" alt="ValueX App Demo" width="300"> </div>

ValueX is an Android app that helps you understand the true cost of products by converting monetary prices into hours of work required to purchase them. Instead of thinking in currency, think in time!
